" #content written_2: "Prof. Ajit Mohan Srivastava is a professor at the Institute of Physics (IOP), Bhubaneswar. His research journey started with a bachelor's degree in Physics at Allahabad University and did his masters at IIT-Kanpur. His doctoral work was in Theoretical High Energy Physics at Syracuse University, New York. He then went on to do his postdoctoral work at The Institute of Theoretical Physics, University of Minnesota and at The Institute for Theoretical Physics, University of California. Prof. Srivastava has a passion for both theoretical and experimental Physics. His research interests include Quark-Gluon Plasma, Gravitational Waves, Cosmic Inflation, Liquid crystal experiments, etc
As someone who has a very keen interest in science communication and student affairs, Prof. Srivastava has been and continues to be a part of multiple science outreach programs and gives frequent talks for students of all backgrounds which are aimed at propagating ideas of learning science and research. He worked as a chairman of the joint IOP/NISER Outreach Committee since 2009. He has been a part of creating Physics Open Discussions for school children at the IOP. He also regularly participates in a joint outreach program with The Institute of Mathematics and Applications, Bhubaneswar. He understands the perspectives of the students and has always emphasised the importance of a student's originality, creativity and freedom of expression in an education system." button_1: Abstract #name of buttons button_2: About the speaker --- {%- include {{ page.format }}.html -%}
