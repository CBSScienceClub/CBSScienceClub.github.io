The question we will explore in this talk is whether we have a similar formula for polynomials of higher degree. A remarkable result of Abel states that such a formula is not possible for an arbitrary polynomial of degree 5.

How does one prove something cannot be done? We will see an approach to such problems, by focussing on the idea behind Abel's proof." #content written_2: "Prof. Ananthnarayan Hariharan is an Associate Professor in the Department of Mathematics, IIT Bombay. He then worked at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln as a Research Assistant Professor before joining the Department of Mathematics at IIT Bombay. He has been known for his refreshing teaching methods among the students and mathematics fraternity.
